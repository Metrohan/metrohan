# 👋 Hi, I'm Metehan Günen

I’m a final-year **Computer Engineering** student focused on building practical systems at the intersection of **AI, backend development, embedded systems, and computer vision**.

Currently, I work as a **Researcher at i-Lab**, where I develop software and AI-driven solutions for real-world healthcare and laboratory workflows. My work spans from **API design and full-stack systems** to **edge-device integrations**, **image processing**, and **medical/biomedical AI applications**.

I enjoy building products that are not only technically solid, but also actually useful in real operational environments.

---

## 🚀 What I’m Working On

- **Biomedical AI & Computer Vision:**  
  Developing AI-assisted systems for surgical instrument analysis, segmentation, tracking, and damage detection.

- **Backend & Full-Stack Systems:**  
  Building scalable platforms, APIs, dashboards, and workflow-driven systems for laboratory and research environments.

- **IoT & Embedded Development:**  
  Working with Raspberry Pi, ESP32, sensors, and device-to-cloud pipelines for real-time data collection and automation.

- **Applied Research & Prototyping:**  
  Turning research ideas into working prototypes that combine software, hardware, and data-driven intelligence.

A platform that aggregates **tech events, bootcamps, hackathons, and training opportunities in Turkey**.  
Built to help students and early-career developers discover valuable opportunities more easily.

**Highlights:**
- Modernized backend with **FastAPI**
- Frontend rebuilt with **React + Vite**
- Responsive design with dark/light theme support
- Improved scraping infrastructure
- Includes a curated **free education resources** section

### Laboratory / Research Platforms

- Data management and reporting

### Surgical Instrument AI Pipeline
An end-to-end system for:
- barcode / QR-based identification
- image capture from edge devices
- cloud data transfer
- segmentation and instrument analysis
- damage detection and reporting

This project combines **computer vision, embedded hardware, backend systems, and practical healthcare workflow design**.
